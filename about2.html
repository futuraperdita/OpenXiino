Overview

OpenXiino is an open-source replacement for the Xiino Data Server. The primary purpose of OpenXiino is to encode images in the proprietary "EBDIMAGE" format. It will also (unintentionally) downgrade HTTPS requests to HTTP, mitigating the fact that Xiino's TLS stack is at least 20 years old and incompatible with the modern web.
